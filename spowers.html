Parenting Superpowers

More Calm. Less Conflict

Get your preschooler's cooperation without threats, bribes, or endless negotiations!

Parenting Superpowers is your practical guide to turning everyday challenges with your preschooler into opportunities for connection and calm. If you've ever felt stuck in power struggles or overwhelmed, this book offers simple, powerful tools to help you parent with more confidence, ease, and joy.

My other book, There Is Always a Reason, helps you understand the “why” behind your preschooler’s behavior. Parenting Superpowers gives you the “what to do next”. Not theories. Tried-and-tested strategies from someone who’s walked alongside thousands of children and their families over three decades, in an environment where power struggles, threats, yelling, time-outs, stern warnings or even raised voices were never an option.

Ever.

Inside: 19 Game-Changing Strategies

Organized into three easy-to-follow superpower categories:

  • Cooperation Superpowers: Win your child’s willingness, not just their compliance.
  • Connection Superpowers: Build trust, safety, and emotional closeness.
  • Foundation Superpowers: Set things up to go smoothly—before tricky moments arise.

Written with warmth, humor, and lived experience, Parenting Superpowers is your real-world guide—no perfection required.
