Quizzes

Try your hand at my parenting quizzes.

See how your own approach aligns with the practices I share in my book—practices that have worked for me over 30 years of teaching preschool-aged children.

Just so you know:

  • Your results are not kept or recorded by me.
  • The quizzes are anonymous.
  • I do not get to see your results.
