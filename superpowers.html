Launches September 1st!

Discover Your Parenting Superpowers

My book There Is Always a Reason helps you understand why your child is behaving a certain way. My new book—Parenting Superpowers—will give you more of the what to do next—concrete, doable, simple strategies to turn everyday power struggles into smoother, more connected moments.

This isn’t theory. It’s the companion book for parents who want to slip smoothly into the role of a calm and confident parent of a preschooler. Inside you’ll discover 19 friendly, down-to-earth “superpowers”—like using positive redirection, binary choices, timers, play-based connection, and environment tweaks—to help your child cooperate more and your home feel less like a battleground.

Written with warmth, humor, and decades of hands-on experience, Parenting Superpowers is your go-to guide for real-life parenting wins—no perfection required.
