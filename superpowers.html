Parenting Superpowers

More Calm. Less Conflict. Every Day

Get your preschooler's cooperation without threats, bribes or endless negotiations!

Parenting Superpowers is your practical guide to turning everyday challenges with your preschooler into opportunities for connection and calm. If you've ever felt stuck in power struggles or overwhelmed, this book offers simple, powerful tools (superpowers!) to help you parent with more confidence, ease, and joy.

There Is Always a Reason, helps you understand the “why” behind your preschooler’s behavior, Parenting Superpowers gives you the “how.” How to get ahead of challenging preschooler behavior. How to prevent it happening. Not theories. Tried-and-tested strategies from someone who’s walked alongside thousands of children and their families over three decades, in an environment where power struggles, threats, yelling, time-outs, stern warnings or even raised voices were never an option. Ever.

More About My Book

Inside Parenting Superpowers you'll find 19 game-changing strategies further organized into three easy-to-follow superpower categories:

  • Cooperation Superpowers: How to win your child’s willingness.
  • Connection Superpowers: How to build trust, safety, and emotional closeness.
  • Foundation Superpowers: How to set things up to go smoothly.

All 19 chapters follow the same structure:

  • Why use the superpower
  • What it is
  • Why it works
  • How to use it
  • Examples of the superpower being used
  • Age-specific approaches
  • What the superpower is best for
  • Common mistakes
  • How you say it matters
  • When it's not working...
  • Building the habit
  • Quick start tips

The book is your practical, real-world guide, written without jargon. You can read it right through, or use it as a reference and dip in an out. The chapters all standalone.

You'll soon find yourself deftly diffusing or deflecting those difficult moments with all the skill and power of a true superhero!
