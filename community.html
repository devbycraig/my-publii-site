The “Between Naps” Community

A Safe Space for Parents of Preschoolers

Parenting preschoolers is rewarding, exhausting, confusing, and sometimes isolating—often all in the same day! You read the books, you try the strategies, but some days you do wonder: Am I doing this right? Is anyone else going through this?

You're not alone. And soon, you won't have to figure it out alone either.

What’s Coming…

I’m creating a private online community to support parents of preschoolers (and especially my readers of There Is Always a Reason and Parenting Superpowers).

A place where you can:

Connect With Other Parents Who “Get It”

  • Share your challenges and victories with others navigating the same stages
  • Get perspective from parents who truly understand what you're experiencing
  • Build genuine connections, without judgment or comparison

Go Deeper With the Content

  • Ask me questions directly about the concepts in my books
  • Discuss real-life applications of the strategies
  • Get support as you implement what you're learning

Find What You Need, Fast

  • Search past conversations for solutions to common challenges
  • Access a growing library of community wisdom
  • No scrolling through endless social media feeds to find the good stuff

Enjoy Peace of Mind

  • Anonymous: Nobody knows who you are
  • Private and safe: Only community members can see what you share
  • Ad-free: No algorithms, no distractions, no data harvesting
  • Uncluttered: Organized, focused conversations that respect your time (preschoolers only nap for so long!)
  • Moderated: A respectful space where we welcome every parent

Why a Paid Community?

A small membership fee helps ensure this remains a committed, engaged community of parents who are serious about understanding their children and growing in their parenting journey. It also keeps the space sustainable, ad-free, and high-quality.

(Exact pricing will be announced at launch but it will be under $10/mth with discounts for annual membership)
